About JoSAA Predictor

Helping JEE aspirants make informed decisions since 2024

Our Mission

Empowering Students with Data-Driven Decisions

Every year, lakhs of students face the daunting task of choosing the right college during JoSAA counseling. With limited time and overwhelming options, many end up making suboptimal choices.

We built JoSAA Predictor to change that. Our tool analyzes official cutoff data from previous years to give you personalized predictions, helping you understand your realistic options and make confident decisions.

Our Data Source

All predictions are based on official JoSAA 2025 cutoff data published after the counseling rounds. We do not modify or estimate any data - what you see is the actual closing ranks from last year's counseling.

Important Note: While historical data is a strong indicator, actual cutoffs for 2026 may vary based on factors like seat matrix changes, number of applicants, and difficulty of the exam.
